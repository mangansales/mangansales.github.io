Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Gains Academy ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and data generated through your use of our services:

  • Identity & contact: name, email address, phone number, social media handles
  • Purchase & billing: transaction records, order history (payment card details are processed by our payment provider and not stored by us)
  • Application & enrolment data: information submitted through forms, strategy call bookings, or onboarding questionnaires
  • Communications: emails, messages, and support correspondence
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages visited, and time spent on site (collected via cookies and analytics tools)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver and manage your purchased programs, coaching, and digital products
  • Process payments and maintain transaction records
  • Communicate regarding your account, enrolment, or support requests
  • Send educational content, program updates, and promotional offers (you may opt out at any time)
  • Improve our website, content, and services through analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations and defend against payment disputes or chargebacks

3. Marketing Communications

By providing your contact details, you consent to receiving marketing emails and messages from Gains Academy. You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us directly. We will not sell your contact information to third parties.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to analyse traffic, remember your preferences, and support advertising. You may adjust cookie settings in your browser, though this may affect site functionality. By continuing to use this site, you consent to our use of cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to operate our business, including payment processors (e.g. Stripe), scheduling software, email marketing platforms, cloud storage, and analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics). These providers only access your data as necessary to perform their services and are required to protect it.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations (including tax and financial records), resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Transaction and purchase records are typically retained for 7 years.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
